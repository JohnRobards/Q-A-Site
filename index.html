Welcome to Washu!


Just starting out at Washington University in St. Louis and not sure of what to do, where to eat, how to meet people with similar interests or what classes you should take? What about what you're going to do with your life or any of the other myriad concerns you might have above living in a new place, possibly for the first time? Well, you're in luck as we hope this FAQ can be a comprehensive list of any questions our new students may have. Think we missed something? Please scroll down to the bottom of the page and either give us a call or submit your question using the button found there for an answer via email.


Washington University in St. Louis strives to be an open and accepting community. One that is built on trust, kindness, and learning. We accept people with a wide variety of interests to our University in the hopes that everyone is able to find their own niche and be able to enjoy themselves while they learn. Whether you're browsing this FAQ because you're a new freshman, foreign exchange student, transfer student, or were just curious about some aspect of life on campus that you didn't know much about, we promise to do our best to address your concerns and help. No matter what your interests or worries are, WashU is here to help, so don't hesitate to ask us or your classmates anything. We all love to help!
